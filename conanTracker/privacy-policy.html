Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Canon Episode Tracker extension operates regarding user data.

Data Collection

This extension does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or sensitive data.

Local Storage

All information related to watched episodes, filter settings, and user preferences is stored locally on your device through Chromeâ€™s local storage system. No information is transmitted to any external servers.

Permissions

This extension uses minimal permissions:

  • Storage: To save your local preferences (e.g., watched episodes).
  • Tabs: To open the tracker page in a new tab when you click the extension icon.
  • Host Access: To read public canon episode information from DetectiveConanWorld.com without modifying or sending any information back.
